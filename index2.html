About

My name is Emily deGrandpré and I am a multi-disciplinary designer with a background in architecture, specializing in user experience and creative research.

Research

I'm interested in ethnographic research, writing, strategy, communication, and complexity in the context of both digital and physical social infrastructures. My core questions involve societal, environmental, and technological shifts.

After graduating from architecture school last spring, I spent summer 2019 working in civic innovation as an intern at Sidewalk Labs. Prior to that, I was working on maps and communication design at Metro21, on brand experiences at Jack Morton, and I also explored an analyst and development role in the context of community solar energy. Many of my expereinces have led me to question: what does a more collaborative, more sustainable, more imaginative future look like?
