Hello! I'm a creative strategist, writer, researcher, dancer and multidisciplinary designer thinking about communication and complexity in the context of both digital and physical spaces. I like to think about the entwinement of community, social infrastrucure, and the processes of change and evolution.

After graduating from architecture school in 2019, I spent the summer working in civic innovation as an intern at Sidewalk Labs. Prior to that, I was working on maps at Metro21, on brand experiences at Jack Morton, and I also explored a sales analyst role in the context of community solar energy. Many of my expereinces have led me to question: what does a more collaborative, more sustainable, more imaginative future look like?

Currently I'm a collaborator at Tiny Factories, Consortia, Are.na, and volunteering as a Notion Pro via New York City and coordinating events for Sustainable Brooklyn.
