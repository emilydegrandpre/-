Hello! I'm a creative strategist and an anti-disciplinarydesigner working within communication and complexity. Some topics of interest include community spaces, social infrastrucure, and the processes of growth and change in both digital and physical environments.

After graduating from carnnegie mellon's architecture school in 2019, I spent the summer researching innovative civic spaces as an intern at Sidewalk Labs. Prior to that, I was worked on maps at Metro21, on brand experiences at Jack Morton, and I also explored a sales analyst role in the context of community solar energy. Many of my expereinces have led me to question... What does a more collaborative, more sustainable, more imaginative future look like?

Currently I'm a collaborator at Tiny Factories, Consortia, Are.na, and volunteering as a Notion Pro via New York City and coordinating events for Sustainable Brooklyn.
