Bonjour! I'm Em, I spend a lot of time ideating, activating, individualizing, leading, and communicating with networks. By networks I mean, groups of people that think about their communities, their creativity, their freedom and their shared futures. I'm under the belief that we need a lot of imagination and art to get where we want to go. My freshman year of college I read a lot of papers that connected the intersection of arts, sciences, and humanities. I read lots about ideas in "The Future of Science...Is Art?, Jonah Lehrer". All this to say, over time I learned that our boundaries and notions of disciplines, are much less concrete than we think. I am learning how to get better at worldbuilding and time management. When I'm not doing that, I commonly translate between 2D & 3D space and like to think about the way that the tangible and the intangible ideas brought into the world inform each other. I seek an understanding of hierarchy and harmony; I'm always grappling with making meaning out of the matter.

You can usually catch me thinking about the root causes of complex or perhaps wicked problems within larger systems and the ways in which a creative practice can start dialogue, start to ask better questions, and to ask how we can provide extraordinary experiences for people.
