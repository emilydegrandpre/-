Bonjour, tu peux m'appeler, Em.

I'm a graduating senior in the School of Architecture at Carnegie Mellon University.

I commonly translate between 2D & 3D space and like to think about the way that the tangible and the intangible ideas brought into the world inform each other. I seek an understanding of hierarchy and harmony; I'm always grappling with making meaning out of the matter.

You can usually catch me thinking about the root causes of complex problems within larger systems and the ways in which a creative practice can start dialogue, start to ask better questions, and to ask how we can provide extraordinary experiences for people.
